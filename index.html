Beautifying Greensboro, NC one website at a time

The GSO Web Initiative is a group of designers, developers, illustrators, graphic artists & photographers working together to provide awesome (aka hip, beautiful & user friendly) websites for local business, artists and entrepreneurs in Greensboro, NC. After seeing one too many buggy and broken websites, we decided to make it our mission to raise the standard of what is an acceptable web presence of our great city. Our vision is to develop collaborative relationships with each business and individual while meeting their online needs and by doing so beautify Greensboro, NC one website at a time.

What is GSO Web Initiative?

The GSO Web Initiative was established in 2012, fueled by a vision to beautify the great city of Greensboro, NC one website at a time. Our mission is to raise the standard of what is an acceptable web presence of our great city. Greensboro, NC is one of the state's most vibrant epicenters of the creative arts and home to an eclectic group of businesses, artists and entrepreneurs. In this web centered culture we believe that Greensboro's online presence should reflect that. Our mission is the make sure it does.

Why GSO Web Initiative?

While perusing the internet & exploring local businesses, we noticed a high number of websites that were out of date, buggy and let's face it, compared to how Greensboro is in real life, kind of boring. Those websites did not represent the city that we know and love and call home and that ruffled our feathers. So we embarked on a journey, an initiative to ensure that when anyone visits a Greensboro, NC website they would be blown away by the design, the graphics, the photography, the functionality, everything and realize how awesome Greensboro, NC really is.
